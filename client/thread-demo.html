Just completed my transition from software engineering to product management! It's been an incredible journey over the past year. For anyone considering a similar career pivot, here are my top 5 lessons learned:

1. Technical background is a huge advantage - Understanding the development process helped me communicate better with engineering teams
2. User research is everything - Spent months learning to conduct interviews and analyze user behavior
3. Data-driven decisions - Moving from code to metrics required a mindset shift
4. Stakeholder management - Balancing different priorities and expectations is an art
5. Strategic thinking - Learning to think beyond features to business impact
